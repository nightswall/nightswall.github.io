Get to know DIONA!

DIONA revolutionizes IoT network security through advanced machine learning techniques. By analyzing IoT device behavior and network traffic in real-time, it detects and mitigates potential threats and anomalies, ensuring unwavering confidentiality and network integrity. Prepare for a new era of IoT protection that pushes the boundaries of security with proactive monitoring and cutting-edge technology.

Demo Video

The video below showcases the practical application of our project, demonstrating how it can be effectively utilized. DIONA, our innovative solution, offers integrated Network Security through Deep Learning, specifically designed for monitoring various applications. By employing a combination of rules and a powerful DL engine, DIONA actively monitors network flow, continuously analyzing the traffic status. Additionally, DIONA extends its monitoring capabilities to IoT telemetries, learning from the collected data and leveraging previous knowledge to make informed decisions and determine behavioral outcomes. What's even more impressive is that DIONA is fully dockerized, enhancing its flexibility and ease of deployment.

Technology

Present in our Stack

Our tech stack includes popular technologies such as Kubernetes, Docker and Python, along with specialized tools like TensorFlow for deep learning and Grafana for data visualization. We also use PyPSA and Pandas data simulation and manipulation for the sake of training of our DL engines. Together, these tools help us build robust and scalable solutions for IoT network security and behavioral analysis.
